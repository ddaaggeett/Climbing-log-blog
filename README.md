# How it Works

In 3 quick steps, share your sends

1\) Ascend your route

2\) Scan the QR tag at the finish hold \(make sure you give your belayer a loud "TAKE"\)

3\) Share your climb with the world \(Twitter, Facebook, etc.\)

GET ON WITH YOUR SESH. THAT'S IT!!!
